David A. Bednar

David A. Bednar is currently the 7th Senior Apostle in the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles. He was born on June 15, 1952 in Oakland, California. His mother was a faithful saint, but his father wasn't a member of the Church. However, he still supported his member family in all that they did. In 1971, he served a mission in Southern Germany.

He was a quarterback for his high school team. He went to BYU, where he got a bachelor's and master's in Communication and Organizational Communication, respectively. After he earned a PhD. in Organizational Behavior at Purdue University. He worked as a professor of business management at Texas Tech University and University of Arkansas. When he was 47, he was called to be the president of Ricks College, where he oversaw the transition of Ricks College to BYU-Idaho.

When he was a quarterback, he met Susan Kae Robinson at one of his games. He was thoroughly impressed when she caught one of throws at a game. They were married at the Salt Lake Temple on March 20, 1975. They have 3 sons together.

On October 2, 2004, he was called to be on the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles.
