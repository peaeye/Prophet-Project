Gary E. Stevenson

Gary E. Stevenson is currently the 12th Senior Apostle in the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles. He was born on August 6, 1955 in Ogden, UT. His family was very virtuous and faithful. His mother was the moral point which he measured everything else against, and his father was the bishop of the local branch. All in all, his family was a major support network for him.

He served on in the Japan Fukuoka mission. While attending USU, he teamed up with another business major to found Weslo Inc., a company that focused on importing Asian kitchenware, tableware, and other products from overseas and sell them in the US. Soon it transitioned into selling wood burning stoves, then into a fitness company, ultimately called ICON Health and Fitness, Inc. With the bachelors of business adminsitration he got from USU, he served as the President and COO until 2008.

He met Lesa Jean Higley in his Old Testament Institute class. They got married at the Idaho Falls Temple in 1979. They had four sons together. He says that her best quality was how she brought a happy, spiritually uplifting feeling into their home and for their family.

On October 3, 2015, he was called to be a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles.
